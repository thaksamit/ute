What we do NOT collect

  • We do not track your behaviour outside UTE
  • We do not use cookies beyond the session token
  • We do not run analytics or advertising trackers
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Third-party services

Your queries are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Anthropic's own privacy policy applies to that processing. We do not store your queries on Anthropic's behalf — they are processed in real time and the response is returned to you.

UTE is hosted on Railway (railway.app). Their infrastructure privacy policy applies to hosting.

Chat history

If you are on the Pro tier, your queries and UTE responses are stored in our database so you can access them later. You can delete any chat at any time from the sidebar. Deleting a chat permanently removes it from our database.

Free tier users' queries are not stored beyond the current session.

Data security

Passwords are never stored in plaintext — we use HMAC-SHA256 with a per-user random salt. Session tokens are cryptographically random 64-character hex strings. All data is stored in SQLite on Railway's infrastructure.

Your rights

You can:

  • Delete your chat history at any time from the sidebar
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing privacy@ute.ai
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us

Children

UTE is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We will update this policy if our practices change. The date at the top of this page reflects the last update. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
